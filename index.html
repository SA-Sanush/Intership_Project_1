Spaghetti Bolognese
Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
Instructions:

Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. In a separate pan, saut√© onions and garlic in olive oil. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes. Serve sauce over spaghetti.

Pancakes
Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
Instructions:

Mix all ingredients to form a smooth batter. Heat a non-stick pan and melt some butter. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook the other side until golden. Serve warm with syrup.

Chicken Curry
Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Curry powder
  • Coconut milk
  • Salt & pepper
Instructions:

Cook onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant, Add chicken and cook until browned, Add tomatoes and curry powder, Stir in coconut milk and simmer for 20-30 minutes, Serve with rice.
